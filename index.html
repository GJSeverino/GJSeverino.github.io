Hello!

I am a PhD student in Cognitive Science at Indiana University Bloomington. Broadly, my research investigates the extent to which we can compare cognitive dynamics across different levels of collective behavior. Can the physical and organizational constraints of cells in a tissue inform the way that we think about the dynamics of collective behavior in animal or social systems? Are we able to see similar consequences and tradeoffs between animals in an ecosystem and cells in a tissue? Furthermore, how do traditional principles of cognition such as learning and memory change as we vary the parameters between these different levels of collectivity? Do the metaphors hold up? Or should they solely be viewed as metaphors?

In order to study this, I use a combination of computational modeling, dynamical systems analysis, and experimental methodologies.
